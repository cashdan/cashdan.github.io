Film Review Assignment

  1. Watch the film Real People, Real Careers. It is a 47 minute film consisting of interviews with anthropologists in a variety careers, at least some of which are likely to surprise you.

    The url to stream the video is on the turnitin calendar for March 10.

    Sorry about the inonvenience of the extra link. I can't put the url here because this is a public website and the video is available only to this class. You can also view the DVD at the library knowledge commons; the call number is GN 41.8 .A59 2006.

  2. Write a brief essay (about 250 words) describing two or more of the careers discussed in the film, focusing on those of greatest interest to you.

    The point of the written assignment is to make it obvious to me that you watched the film, so include enough specifics to accomplish that. In other words, for the careers you describe, include in your discussion a description of what the anthropologist in the film is doing, not just a review of the field they are working in.

    Submit to turnitin.

OPTIONAL: Marriott has other films about anthropology careers that you might find of interest, including:
  • Anthropologists at Work: Careers Making a Difference (GN 41.8 A58 2000z). This is a 41-minute film featuring interviews with applied anthropologists in the four sub-fields (cultural anthropology, linguistics, physical anthropology, and archaeology) who are using anthropology to make the world a better place.

  • Beyond Ethnography: Corporate and Design Anthropology (GN 345 .B49 2007). The film draws on ethnographic research projects conducted at General Motors Corporation and offers a successful model for the use of anthropology in corporate settings.
